We embarked on a delightful day trip, departing from Philadelphia and heading towards the excitement of Atlantic City.

Hard Rock Hotel & Casino

Our first stop was the Hard Rock Hotel & Casino, a place pulsating with energy and entertainment, offering a glimpse into the glamorous side of New Jersey.

Lunch at

As noon approached, we set off for the charming town of Cape May, renowned for its picturesque streets and historical significance. Here, we indulged in a hearty lunch at the Ugly Mug Bar & Restaurant, a local favorite known for its welcoming atmosphere and delicious fare.

Washington Street Mall

Post-lunch, we strolled down Washington Street Mall in downtown Cape May, a vibrant area brimming with unique shops and historic charm. This shopping haven offered us a perfect blend of retail therapy and architectural beauty.

Cape May Point State Park

The highlight of our trip was the visit to Cape May Point State Park, particularly the historic Cape May Lighthouse. Built in 1859, this iconic structure has guided ships through the treacherous waters off New Jersey for over two centuries【40†source】. The experience of climbing the lighthouse and soaking in the panoramic views of the Atlantic Ocean and Delaware Bay was truly unforgettable.
